# Malaria Treatment Guidelines

## Uncomplicated Malaria

- First-line: Artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT) for uncomplicated Plasmodium falciparum.
- Ensure a positive diagnostic test (microscopy or RDT) before treatment when possible.

## Severe Malaria

- Immediate parenteral therapy and urgent referral to higher-level care.
- Monitor for complications and provide supportive care.
